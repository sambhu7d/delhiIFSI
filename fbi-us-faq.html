FBI (US)

FAQ

If you want to retrieve a police clearance certificate or background check from FBI, we can help! We at Delhi Fingerprint have helped thousands of applicants to retrieve their criminal record check from FBI through the normal mode as well as the fast mode.

  • I need my US PCC from FBI. How can you help?

    Just take an appointment and visit us at the appointed time. Our trained technicians would record your fingerprints on the standard FD-258 card and guide you through the complete application.

  • Will you take care of sending the documents to FBI also?

    Yes, we will take care of sending all the required documents to the FBI.

  • How much time does it take to get my Identity History Summary Check/Criminal Background check/ Police clearance certificate directly from the FBI?

    Around 12-14 weeks plus mailing time.

  • How do you expedite the processing of my application reducing the wait time to receive the certificate from 12-14 weeks to 10-12 days?

    We have a FBI-approved Channel partner who will receives the fingerprint card, requisite documents and associated fees. Our Channel partner will electronically forward the fingerprint submission with the necessary information to the FBI, Criminal Justice Information Services Division Attention: Clarksburg, West Virginia 26306, U.S.A. Our Channel partner will expedite the application and will mail out the results to you after receiving the record check.

  • What documents do I need to bring to your office for availing your services?

    You need to bring a copy of your photo identification proof like Passport, Driving licence, Pan card, Voter card, Aadhar card or any other valid photo ID proof.

  • My attorney in US has asked for a completed fingerprint form FD-258. Can you help me?

    We provide professional fingerprinting services at a reasonable cost ensuring the acceptability of your fingerprints by the FBI.

  • Is it required for me to print the FD-258 or do you have it printed in your office?

    We have all the requisite form available with us in our office.

  • I am asked to submit duly legalized police clearance certificate from the country in I have last resided i.e. US. Can you help me with this?

    Yes we will help you to obtain police clearance certificate from FBI.

  • I need to get only my finger printing on the Form FD-258?

    Just take an appointment and visit us at the appointed time. Our trained fingerprint expert would record your fingerprints on the standard FD-258 card and give the filled up cards.

  • Can fingerprints be taken at my doorstep?

    Our fingerprints expert can visit your home or office to take your fingerprints.

  • Do I need to affix a return envelope with postage paid to be posted to FBI?

    No, it is not required.

  • Do they accept payment by credit card?

    Sorry! We accept only cash.
