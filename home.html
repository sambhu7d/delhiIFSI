You would require a PCC from RCMP (Canada) when:-

  • You want to become a permanent resident of Canada.
  • You have made an application for Canadian citizenship.
  • You are not a citizen of Canada but if you have lived in Canada and now you want to immigrate, work, obtain a visa or acquire the citizenship of any other country, US immigration visa.
  • If you require it to be used for the Department of Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship.
  • If you require it for Immigrating to a country other than Canada.
  • If you require it for Immigrating to Canada.
  • If your Company requires you to undergo Employment Screening.
  • If you want to retrieve a good conduct certificate.
  • In all of these cases you and your family members require to submit a police certificate.
