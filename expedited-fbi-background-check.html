Expedited FBI Background Check

Procedure

Procedure for obtaining USA PCC (criminal history records check from CJIS, FBI)/ Criminal History Check or Criminal History Record Check in only 10-12 days while living in Delhi:-

  1. Just call us at: +91-9968989548 OR mail us at contact@indiafingerprinting.in and take an appointment.
  2. Visit us at the appointed time with a valid photo ID. We have all the requisite forms available with us, so you need not worry!
  3. Our experts will cater to all your fingerprinting needs and send all your documents to the FBI. We take care of the complete application process. You just need to give your fingerprints.
  4. The complete process of fingerprinting and all other formalities would take only 15-20 minutes.
  5. You will receive your Criminal Record Check/Identity History Summary Check report (hard copy) within 10-12 business days.

Procedure for obtaining USA PCC (criminal history records check from CJIS, FBI) in only 10-12 days while living in any other state:-

  1. Just call us at: +91-9968989548 OR mail us at contact@indiafingerprinting.in and take an appointment.
  2. Our representative would help you to reach our office nearby your place where you can get your fingerprinting done.
  3. Send the completed fingerprint card (FD-258 Card) to our Delhi head office.
  4. We would process your application on the same day we receive it. We would also take care of the FBI’s fee of US $18 as well.
  5. You will receive your Criminal Record Check/Identity History Summary Check report (hard copy) within 10-12 business days.

*BE AWARE THAT THE FBI OR ITS APPROVED CHANELLERS MAIL OUT THE POLICE CHECK CERTIFICATE BY POST ONLY. THE POLICE CHECK CERTIFICATE IS HIGHLY CONFIDENTIAL AND PERSONAL AND CANNOT BE DISCLOSED TO A THIRD PARTY. THE AGENCIES WHO CLAIM TO PROVIDE A SCANNED COPY ARE FRAUD.
